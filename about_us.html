About Us


Our Motivation

Have you ever placed an order online and wondered when it would arrive or if it would even be the right items? You're not alone.

At KTM SuperMart, we take our customers’ concerns seriously. That’s why we’ve worked diligently to create a seamless online grocery shopping experience. Our goal is to ensure that ordering online is as effortless and convenient as it should be. With KTM SuperMart Online, we’ll deliver everything you need, exactly when you need it, fresh to your doorstep.


About KTM SuperMart Online

KTM SuperMart Online is the digital extension of KTM SuperMart, located in Hikkaduwa. Our platform allows customers to browse a wide variety of products, including fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy, meat, fish, and much more. Whether you’re in Sri Lanka or abroad, you can easily place an order for yourself or send groceries to your loved ones.

We are committed to delivering your orders promptly to the areas we serve, ensuring that your shopping experience is stress-free. With flexible payment options, including cash on delivery, we make it easy for you to get what you need, when you need it.

We’re trusted by customers both locally and internationally, and have been serving you with dedication through our physical stores. Now, with KTM SuperMart Online, we continue our commitment to quality and service, ensuring you receive the best, 365 days a year.

Accessible across web, iOS, and Android platforms, KTM SuperMart Online is here to serve you better and faster, staying true to our motto: Groceries at Your Convenience.
